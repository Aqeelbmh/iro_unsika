Student Exchange Program

Experience studying abroad with our partner universities worldwide.

Program Overview

Our Student Exchange Program offers Unsika students the opportunity to study at partner universities around the world for one or two semesters. This program enhances cultural understanding, academic excellence, and global networking.

About Student Exchange

This program allows you to immerse yourself in a new culture, gain a global perspective, and build an international network, all while earning credits towards your Unsika degree.

Study for 1-2 semesters abroad
Credits transfer back to Unsika
Cultural immersion experience
No additional tuition fees at partner university

Available Destinations

  • Japan - Tokyo University of Technology
  • South Korea - Seoul National University
  • Thailand - Mahidol University
  • Malaysia - Universiti Teknologi MARA
  • Singapore - National University of Singapore

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with required documents including academic transcripts, recommendation letters, and a motivation statement.

2

Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview to assess their motivation, academic performance, and language proficiency.

3

Selection & Placement

Successful candidates will be matched with partner universities based on their academic interests and available programs.

4

Pre-departure Orientation

Attend orientation sessions covering cultural preparation, academic requirements, and practical arrangements for your study abroad period.
