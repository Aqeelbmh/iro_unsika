Scholarship Programs

UNSIKA offers various scholarship opportunities to support international students and help make quality education accessible to talented individuals from around the world.

Full and partial tuition coverage
Living allowance support
Merit-based and need-based awards
Research and academic excellence awards

Available Scholarships

UNSIKA Excellence Scholarship

Full tuition coverage for outstanding international students

Merit-Based Scholarship

Partial tuition coverage based on academic performance

Research Scholarship

Support for research-focused students

Need-Based Grant

Financial assistance for students with demonstrated need

Eligibility Criteria

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 (out of 4.0)
  • Strong academic record
  • Research experience (for research scholarships)
  • Leadership and extracurricular activities

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement and motivation letter
