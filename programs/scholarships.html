Scholarships

Access various scholarship opportunities for international education.

Funding Your Studies

Unsika is committed to making quality education accessible. We offer various scholarship opportunities to support international and domestic students, recognizing academic excellence, leadership potential, and financial need. Explore the options available to help you achieve your academic goals.

Types of Scholarships

We provide a range of scholarships to cater to the diverse needs of our student community. Whether you are an outstanding academic performer, a research-focused student, or in need of financial support, there may be a scholarship for you.

Full and partial tuition coverage
Living allowance and stipend support
Merit-based and need-based awards

Available Scholarships

Unsika Excellence Scholarship

Full tuition coverage for outstanding international students.

Merit-Based Scholarship

Partial tuition coverage based on academic performance.

Research Scholarship

Funding for students engaged in research projects.

Need-Based Grant

Financial assistance for students with demonstrated need.

Eligibility & Application

General Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 on a 4.0 scale (or equivalent).
  • Proof of English proficiency (e.g., TOEFL, IELTS).
  • Strong academic record and letters of recommendation.
  • Demonstrated leadership and extracurricular involvement.

Required Documents

  • Completed scholarship application form.
  • Official academic transcripts and certificates.
  • Personal statement or motivation letter.
  • Passport copy and other identification documents.
